Healthy Bees

The Bees that communicate are called Healthy Bees. All Healthy Bees will be in green color and when a Bee is not communicating, it is displayed in red.

On latest Firmware

If a Bee is Healthy and on the latest Firmware, it will be in green color. If a Bee is not on the latest Firmware the Firmware Rev cell will be in orange color. You can manange the latest Firmwares here

Ready to ship

A Bee is ready to ship if it is Healthy, assigned to Roambee account and has more than 70% battery charge. If not, the cells will be in orange.
